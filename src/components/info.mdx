<Heading variant="h2" mt="3">Overview</Heading>

<Divider color="text" mb="4" />

This app allows you to make Google Calendar events more quickly by parsing a simple shorthand notation that looks like this:

<Code type="pre">
    t, d, 2w, 3m, 4y buy some bananas
</Code>

This will create the same event today (<Code>t</Code>), in one day (<Code>d</Code>), in two weeks (<Code>2w</Code>), in three months (<Code>3w</Code>) and in four years (<Code>4y</Code>). But it won't send the event to Google Calendar yet, it will add it to the review area.

You can now press the enter key if you want to add additional events to the review area.

You can also re-add Recent Events to your review area by clicking on them.

In the review area, you can choose to delete or send events.

To view the events you've created in Google Calendar, you can <Link href={process.env.GCAL_PUBLIC_CALENDAR} target="_blank">open this public calendar</Link>.

(Click the **Info** button on the top-right at any time to re-open this dialogue.)
